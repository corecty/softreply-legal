AI Policy

SoftReply is a support tool that uses AI to rewrite text entered by users. Outputs are reference drafts only. Please review them yourself before sending or using them.

Input Precautions

Do not enter personal information, confidential information, credentials, payment information, medical information, legal consultation details, or third-party private information.

Outputs

AI-generated outputs may be unnatural, inaccurate, inappropriate, or different from your intent. Please carefully review messages that may affect relationships, work, or legal matters.

Prohibited Use

You must not use the Service for threats, harassment, discrimination, strengthening insults, stalking, self-harm or harm to others, professional legal/medical/financial advice, or attempts to silence or defeat another person in a conflict.

Quality Improvement

We may review inputs, outputs, selected options, and internal evaluation logs for quality improvement, debugging, abuse prevention, and manual review of public candidates.
