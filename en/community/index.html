Community Guidelines

SoftReply may later publish example rewrites submitted or generated by users, only after manual review by the operations team. Published content must not include personal or confidential information.

Content We Do Not Publish

We do not publish content containing identifiable personal information, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, social media accounts, workplaces, schools, client names, credentials, payment information, medical information, legal consultation details, or other confidential information.

We do not publish content involving discrimination, threats, harassment, defamation, sexual exploitation, encouragement of self-harm or harm to others, or assistance with illegal conduct.

Manual Review

Public candidates are never published automatically. We manually review safety, anonymity, and expression before publication.

Removal

We may remove or hide published content at any time if we determine it is inappropriate.
